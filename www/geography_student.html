Lesson Plan

Goals:

Students will be able to:

  1. Understand the definitions of bodies of water.
  2. Understand the definitions of land masses.
  3. Identify new terms and their definitions.

Objectives:

After completing this section, the student will state the bodies of water. Students will also be able to state the various land masses. They will compare and contrast bodies of water and land masses with complete accuracy.


Materials:

Geography Text Book, pictures, worksheets


Introduction:

Chapter 3: Section 2.1 & 2.2 Introduce new terms to students through the use of an overhead projector, iPads, apps, videos, and textbooks. Use each word in a sentence to clarify terms. Students will listen to explanation by the teacher. Teacher will encourage the students to use thinking skills in discussion and questioning.


Development:

Teacher will use textbooks, iPads, apps and videos for students. Students will observe, discuss and use hands on techniques to learn the the bodies of water and land masses.


Practice:

Students will work in pairs to practice identification of bodies of water and land masses using iPads, apps, and worksheets.
