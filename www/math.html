Lesson Plan

Goals:

Students will be able to:

  1. Understand the definitions of different triangle types.
  2. Understand different angles and their relationship to triangles.
  3. Identify new terms and their definitions.

Objectives:

After completing this section, the student will state types of triangles. Students will also be able to state the various angles. They will identify new terms and definitions with complete accuracy.


Materials:

Math textbook, worksheets, iPad


Introduction:

Chapter 4: Section 4.1 & 4.2 Introduce new terms to students through the use of video on iPads and instruction, worksheets, and textbooks. Complete worksheets to clarify terms. Students will listen to explanation by the teacher and watch video. Teacher will encourage the students to use thinking skills in discussion and questioning.


Development:

Teacher will use textbooks, worksheets, iPads, and videos for students. Students will observe, discuss and use hands on techniques to learn the various classifications of triangles and angles.


Practice:

Students will complete homework to practice identification of different angles and classification of triangles.
